# Traduttore Pro — User Guide

Welcome to your translation management platform. This guide explains everything you need to know to use your website.

---

## How the website works

Your website has two parts:

- **Homepage** — where your clients submit translation requests
- **Admin Panel** — where you manage and respond to those requests

---

## Accessing the admin panel

1. Open your website in the browser
2. Go to `yourwebsite.com/admin`
3. Enter your username and password
4. You will see your dashboard with all incoming requests

> Keep your username and password private. Never share them with anyone.

---

## Managing translation requests

When a client submits a request from the homepage, it will appear in your dashboard under **Pending Requests**.

Each request shows:
- Client name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Type of project (Legal, Medical, Business, etc.)
- Translation type (Arabic → Italian, etc.)
- Their message

---

## Approving a request

1. Find the request you want to approve
2. Click the **date field** next to the request and pick a meeting date
3. Click **Approve & Send Email**
4. The client will automatically receive a confirmation email with the meeting date

> You must select a date before approving — the system will remind you if you forget.

---

## Rejecting a request

1. Find the request you want to reject
2. Click **Reject**
3. The client will automatically receive a polite email letting them know

---

## Filtering requests

At the top of the dashboard you will find filter tabs:

- **All** — shows every request
- **Arabic → Italian**
- **Italian → Arabic**
- **French → Italian**
- **English → Italian**

Click any tab to see only requests of that type.

---

## The stats bar

At the top of your dashboard you will always see:

| Stat | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Total | All requests received |
| Pending | Waiting for your response |
| Approved | Confirmed with a meeting date |
| Rejected | Declined requests |

---

## Emails sent to your clients

Your platform sends two types of automatic emails:

**When you approve:**
> Dear [Client Name], your translation request has been approved. Meeting Date: [date]. Please come to our office with your original documents.

**When you reject:**
> Dear [Client Name], thank you for contacting Traduttore Pro. Unfortunately we are unable to process your request at this time.

All emails are sent from your Gmail account automatically — you do not need to do anything manually.

---

## Signing out

When you are done, click **Sign Out** in the top right corner of the dashboard.

---

## If something goes wrong

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Cannot log in | Check your username and password — make sure Caps Lock is off |
| Email not received by client | Ask them to check their spam folder |
| Page not loading | Refresh the browser and try again |
| Any other issue | Contact your developer: Yousri Chaibi |
